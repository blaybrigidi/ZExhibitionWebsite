Meet Zahra

Zahra Faye is an art curator and storyteller dedicated to preserving and celebrating African heritage through exhibitions, workshops, and storytelling. Her work empowers communities by connecting past rituals with modern practices, highlighting heritage as a source of empowerment, innovation, and collective healing.
